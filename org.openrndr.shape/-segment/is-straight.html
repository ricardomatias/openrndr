isStraight

fun isStraight(epsilon: Double = 0.01): Boolean (source)

Determines if the Segment forms a straight line.

If the given Segment has control points, the function verifies that they do not add any curvature to the path.

Parameters

epsilon - The margin of error for what's considered a straight line.
